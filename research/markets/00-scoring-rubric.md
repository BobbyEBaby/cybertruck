# Scoring rubric

Every opportunity in this folder is scored on four axes, each 1–5 (5 = best). Total out of 20.

| Axis | 1 | 5 |
|---|---|---|
| **Time-to-first-dollar** | Months | Days |
| **$0 viability** | Needs paid hosting/assets/ads | Free everything, end-to-end |
| **Automation-friendliness** | Requires ongoing human creativity & negotiation | Agent can do most of it; human is just upload + payout |
| **Ceiling** | Caps below $1k total | Could plausibly clear five figures with one hit |

A score is just a starting heuristic. Real signal comes from actually shipping and measuring.

## Honest disclaimers baked into every entry
- Numbers cited are seeded from prior knowledge (cutoff May 2025) and **must** be refreshed via WebSearch by task B-004 once the schedule is running.
- "Plausibly could earn X" is not a forecast. It is the upper bound of *known examples*. Most attempts in any category earn $0.
- Categories that require sustained human creativity (faceless YouTube voiceovers, influencer content) score lower on automation-friendliness even if their ceiling is high.
