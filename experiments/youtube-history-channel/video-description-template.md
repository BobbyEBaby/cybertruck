## Notes on the template (not part of the actual description — strip before uploading)

**Why 4 tip rails visible at once:** different viewers prefer different rails. Ko-fi appeals to a creator-class audience; crypto appeals to a different audience that specifically wants to tip in crypto; Gumroad appeals to people who want something tangible for their money. Showing all 4 at once doesn't dilute — it lets each viewer self-select.

**Why the XRP destination tag is inline and flagged:** XRP deposits without the destination tag are lost forever. The flag has to be impossible to miss. Do not remove this line.

**Why no BMC in the top 2 spots:** Ko-fi is 0% fee, BMC is 5% fee. Prefer Ko-fi for tippers who read top-to-bottom. BMC is listed for audience reach.

**Rotating CTA placement in the narration:** mention a *different* single tip rail at the end of each video (not all 4 — that sounds like an ad read). Rotate: video 1 = Ko-fi, video 2 = "crypto link in the description," video 3 = Ko-fi, video 4 = "PDF deep dives linked below." Measure which one converts best and weight accordingly after ~10 videos.

**Wikimedia + LoC credit compliance:** Library of Congress public domain items require no attribution, but Wikimedia CC-BY and CC-BY-SA items DO. Track per image in `assets/<video-slug>/sources.md` and publish the full list as a pinned comment on the video (YouTube descriptions have a 5,000-char limit — long attribution lists go in the pinned comment instead).
